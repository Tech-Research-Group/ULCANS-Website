REPAIR USING CABLE TIES (small tears)

NOTE: If cable ties are not available, use twine, cord, string, or lightweight rope.

1. Remove debris and ragged material from damaged area of screen.

NOTE: Position cable ties 1 in apart and 1 in (2.54cm) away from edge of tear.

2. Starting at one end of tear, push cable tie through net material.

3. Position cable tie perpendicular to tear. Push through net material on other side of tear.

4. Close cable tie and tighten. Ensure material is tightly secured to prevent loose material or gaps.

5. Repeat Steps 2-4 until tear is sealed. Cut off excess cable ties.



REPAIR USING REPAIR MATERIAL (large tears)

1. Remove debris and ragged material from damaged area of screen.

2. Select a piece of screen material from the screen repair kit of the correct type and class.

NOTE: Repair material should be slightly larger than repair area.

3. Cut repair material to appropriate size.

4. Position repair material over damaged screen. Attach using the Repair Using Cable Ties procedure.
