PROCEDURES - STRIKE

Previous | Next

RETRIEVAL - 2

  1. Remove all tent pins (1) securing net assembly.
  2. Wipe mud/dirt from tent pins and telescopic poles (2).
  3. Stow telescopic poles in support system carrying bag (3).
  4. Attach spanner wrench (4) to tent pin carrying case (5).
  5. Place tent pins in carrying case. Place case in support system carrying bag.
  6. Place hammer (6) in support system bag.
  7. Spread net assembly over level ground and remove leaves, twigs, dirt, or foreign objects from screens.
  8. Repair screens as necessary. See Maintenance for additional information.
