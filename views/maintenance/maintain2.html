BECKET LOOP REPAIR

  • A missing or broken becket loop may be bypassed by looping through next available becket loop.
  • When more than one becket loop in a row is missing or broken, replace them using available tie material.
  • If three or more of the same kind of loops (short, long) in a row are damaged or missing, replace screen.
